Biography

Hon. (Dr.) Godwin T. Oritogun was the Minority Leader in thw Kogi Sate House of Assembly (1999-2003). He obtained Doctor in Veterinary Medicine (DVM) from AHmadu Bello University, Zaria. He also bagged Masters in Business Administration (MBA) from Nnamdi Azikwe University, Akwa. He held a Diploma in Computer Studies. After graduation fom the university, he served with the Ministry of Agriculture and Natural Resources, Sokoto.

He was a Lecturer at the College of Agriculture and Animal Science, Ahmadu Bello University, Mando Road, Kaduna. He was employed as a sales Representative for the Western and Eastern Zones by Dalam Nigeria Limited/Roche Nigeria Limited. After he retired from public and private jobs, he managed his own business as Managing Director of Amad'Osi Business Enterprises and General Manager of Tekebale Nigeria Limited.

In 1999, Dr. Oritogun contested and won election into the kogi state House of Assembly to represent Ogori/Mangogo Constituency.During his tenure in the Assembly, he was afflicted with a strange renal disease from which Jesus miraculously delivered him. The government of Alh. Ibrahim idris appointed him the Chairman of the Kogi State Teaching Service Commission (TSC) (August 2 - December 24, 2004)

Hon. (Dr) G. T. Oritogun was an Author, Song Writer, Script Writer, Actor, Preacher and trvaelled all around the world. He was happily married with Children. He was called to a Glorious Eternal Rest on December 24, 2004. This is what he wanted to be written on his tombstone:

"Here lives the man that proved that they that put their trust in the Lord shall not be put to shame"
